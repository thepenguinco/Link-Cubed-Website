About Us

Link Cubed, a proud Junior Achievement company, specializes in decorative and handmade products, made from reclaimed wood, that not only serve as fine art pieces, but also gives back to the environment. For every ten products you buy, one tree will be planted. Come join us on our mission to give back to the environment, one tree at a time!
